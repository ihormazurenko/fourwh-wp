We’re Hiring

Four Wheel Campers is hiring for several different positions here at our factory in Woodland, CA. We are looking for more hard workers to help us build some of the best pop-up trucks campers available in the USA.

Haven't worked in a manufacturing environment before? No big deal. Will will teach & train the right people. Do you like to work with your hands? Perfect, because that is what we are all about.   • Quality Control / Final Inspector (inspecting new campers to make sure everything looks go od and was installed correctly).
